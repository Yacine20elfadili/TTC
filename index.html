Who are we?

TTC is a premier tennis club in Taroudant that offers diverse sporting activities.
Our facility features professional tennis courts, a fully-equipped gym,
and two ping-pong tables for table tennis enthusiasts. We provide a comprehensive
environment for both tennis training and fitness development, making us a complete
sports destination for our members.
